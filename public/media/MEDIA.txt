  neuropod/           → neuropod.mov or preview.mp4 — iPhone mock on hover

  share-on/           → shareon.MP4 or preview.mp4 — iPhone mock on hover

Git / deploy notes:
  • Videos under 100MB can be committed (solo, neuropod, share-on, atlas).
  • vinylmusicplayer.mov is ~231MB — gitignored; compress or upload to Vercel manually.
  • To shrink a recording: ffmpeg -i in.mov -vcodec h264 -crf 28 -preset slow out.mp4
